How to read this scorecard

Every shop is graded on 4 metrics. The number in each cell is your actual selling number for that metric. Below each column header is the best shop in your tier on that metric — that's the bar to beat. The Total Score column is your overall rank inside your tier — goal is 100%. The #1 shop in each tier wins.
Suit Completion25% weight
Of every 100 customers who buy any suit piece (blazer or pant), how many leave with the complete pair.
Shirt-to-Suit25% weight
Of every 100 customers who buy a blazer, how many also leave with a dress shirt.
Accessory-to-Suit15% weight
Of every 100 customers who buy a blazer, how many also buy any accessory (ties, belts, pocket squares, cufflinks, shoes, socks).
AOV35% weight
Average dollars sold per transaction.
Methodology · all metrics are in-store invoices, full selected month, Shopify POS export, US + Canada combined.
Currency: Canadian shops (Toronto, Oakville) converted to USD at the live Bank of Canada / ECB rate.
Scoring: each metric's grade = (shop's value ÷ tier's best on that metric) × 100, capped at 100. Total Score = weighted sum, calibrated within tier. Weights: Suit Completion 25% · Shirt-to-Suit 25% · Accessory-to-Suit 15% · AOV 35%.
Suit definition: any in-store invoice containing a blazer (Product type = Suit). Pant bucket: Pants + Dress Pants. Shirt bucket: Shirt + Classic Fit. Accessory bucket: Ties, Belts, Pocket Squares, Accessory (cufflinks/studs/etc.), Shoes, Socks.
Excluded: Tailoring Tile, Wedding, Backorder, Sample Sale, Swatch, RUSH FEE. Locations excluded: Sample Product POS, bare Ann Arbor (warehouse), West Hollywood POS.
Tiering: shops grouped by monthly USD net sales. #1 in each tier earns $250 toward a team outing.
